# Carica papaya — raw

**Identity:** mīkana, papaya, pawpaw. Caricaceae. PSF role: sub-canopy / fruit. Copy entry #7.
The gap crop — fruit in six months, spent by year four.

**Succession (axis 2):** **placenta 2** · lifespan 3–4 yr productive (fruit from 6 mo, spent by yr 4).
Götsch's own example of a placenta fruit — the gap-filler that says goodbye, not a permanent tree.

## Climate & site — by source
- **Rainfall** — Ferns: preferred **1,500–2,500 mm → 59–98 in**; tolerated **1,000–3,000 mm → 39–118 in**.
  (Shipped `60–100 in` pref / `40–120 in` tol — rounds Ferns, confirmed.)
- **Temperature** — Ferns: preferred **21–30 °C → 70–86 °F**; tolerated **12–44 °C → 54–111 °F**;
  **killed at −1 °C → 30 °F**. (Shipped "70 to 86°F, dies at 30°F", confirmed. No frost.)
- **Altitude** — Ferns: best below **900 m → ~2,950 ft**; up to **2,100 m** near the equator.
  (Shipped `coast to 3,000 ft` = the 900 m production ceiling, confirmed.)
- **Soil / pH** — Ferns: deep, humus-rich, **well-drained and well-aerated** — waterlogged, it dies
  within days. pH pref 5.5–7, tol 4.5–8.
- **Light** — Ferns: sunny; shelter from wind (hollow trunk goes over).

## Propagation & timing — by source
- Ferns: seed, germinates **2–6 weeks at 24–30 °C**; greenwood cuttings, grafting. Crops from **6
  months old** (shipped `food:6`). Fruit maturation **150–240 days**. Plants come up male /
  female / hermaphrodite; ~1 male serves 6+ females.
- **Lifespan** — Ferns: highest yields years 2–3; **declined sharply by year 4.**

## Uses / food — by source
- Ripe as fruit; green as a vegetable (most of Asia). Seeds a mustard-cress spice. Male flowers a
  bitter green; young leaves cooked. Old leaves carry carpaine (left alone). Latex = papain.

## Hazards — by source
- Old leaves (carpaine) not eaten. Green-fruit latex stings a cut. Otherwise none of note.
