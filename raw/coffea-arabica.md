# Coffea arabica — raw

**Identity:** kope, coffee. Rubiaceae. PSF role: sub-canopy / bean (beverage). Copy entry #15.
The understory crop the whole Götsch system was worked out on, under Inga.

**Succession (axis 2):** **secondary 2 · understory** · lifespan 30–40 yr economic (to 70). Evolved
under a canopy and still wants one — planted *into* the nurse's shade, never open ground.

## Climate & site — by source
- **Rainfall** — Ferns: preferred **1,400–2,300 mm → 55–91 in**; tolerated **750–4,200 mm → 30–165 in**.
  (Shipped `55–90 in` pref / `30–165 in` tol, confirmed.)
- **Temperature** — Ferns: preferred **14–28 °C → 57–82 °F**; tolerated **10–34 °C → 50–93 °F**.
  (Shipped "57 to 82°F" — the mild band, not the hot coast. Confirmed.)
- **Altitude** — Ferns: varies with latitude — at ~15° minimum **~500 m → ~1,640 ft**; equatorial
  optimum 1,500–1,900 m. (Shipped "above ~1,600 ft, up to 5,000" — the 1,600 ft floor is the ~500 m
  latitude-adjusted minimum that puts it in the Kona belt; confirmed.)
- **Soil / pH** — Ferns: deep, friable (not stiff clay or sand); **tolerant of acid soils**; pH 5.5–7,
  tol 4.3–8.4.
- **Light** — Ferns: **light shade** — it evolved under a canopy and still wants one.

## Propagation & timing — by source
- Ferns: seed within 2 months of harvest, germinated warm (30–32 °C); nursery 6–12 months. First
  bearing **2–3 years**, full **6–8 years** (shipped `first crop year 2–3`).
- **Lifespan** — Ferns: economic life **30–40 years** on average (range 10–70).

## Uses / food — by source
- Ripe red cherry → dried, roasted, ground, brewed bean. Nothing about it is quick or optional.

## Hazards — by source
- None of note (as prepared).

## Inferences
- The Kona cloud that rolls in each afternoon *is* the shade tree — a lived siting fact that matches
  Ferns' light-shade requirement without any hand-planted canopy. *[Inference from habit + local geography.]*

## Anecdotes
- Grown on small family parcels — unusual for coffee anywhere in the world. Arrived 1828. *[Texture.]*
