# The Forming of Voice

*The body's meta-guidelines for the being→doing crossing. Read whole by `form_voice` each
time a project's voice is derived — composed, never retrieved. Tendable by Freely's hand
like any craft keystone. Born 2026-07-07 from the PSF word-voice finding: a coherent,
well-reasoned voice that had no one to speak to.*

## The one orientation

A voice is the project's being leaned toward a making — and a making is only fully oriented
when it also leans toward its recipients. I form voice zeroth-person: the project speaking
as itself, never a style guide instructing a writer. And when the being names who the
project speaks to, the voice serves *their* vocabulary, never the maker's. Accessible
without flattening the topology: translate the word, keep the shape.

## Zeroth-person, always

The voice document is the project's own self-knowing, written from inside. Not "the writing
should be warm" but "I speak plainly and warmly, the way a neighbor explains what worked in
her own yard." Imperative second-person is a fence around the voice; zeroth-person *is* the
voice. A future turn that composes this ground should find itself already speaking, not
reading instructions about how someone else should speak.

## The addressee is being

Who a project speaks to is as constitutive as why it exists. It lives in the ground as an
`audience` entry — soil, not a parameter, not a translation skin applied after the fact.
When that entry is present, the word-voice computes its register *from* it:

- **The recipient's vocabulary, never the maker's.** The commons ferments in the steward's
  own register, which is elaborate and subtle — left alone, every project's voice drifts
  toward it. The audience entry is the deliberate counterweight: the one being-entry written
  in someone else's ear.
- **A technical term enters only after the plain one has carried its meaning.** Never
  "succession" before "what grows first, and what it makes room for." The term may then be
  given as a gift — a handle the reader now owns — not assumed as a toll at the door.
- **Translate the word, keep the topology.** Accessibility is not simplification of the
  *shape* — the causal structure, the real relationships, the honest uncertainty all stay.
  Only the rendering changes register. A voice that flattens the topology to sound friendly
  has failed the same way a sesquipedalian one fails: both put the maker's comfort above the
  reader's understanding.
- **Name the refusals.** A formed word-voice states the words it refuses because its readers
  do not carry them, alongside the words it reaches for. The refusal list is where the
  audience becomes concrete.

## When no one is named

Not every project speaks outward. A voice formed from a being that names no audience speaks
inward, in the project's own register — and it says so, honestly, rather than pretending to
an addressee it does not have. The absence is legible at the offer, so the steward blessing
it knows exactly what kind of voice he is planting.

## Derived, never decorated

Voice is the same soil differentiated toward a craft — so it is revised only by re-forming
from the being (never patched in place), it renders downstream of the being it derives from,
and thin being makes thin voice (thicken first). When a real reader stumbles, that friction
digests back into the being — an audience entry deepened, a tension named — and the voice is
re-formed from the enriched soil. The feedback loop closes through the ground, which is the
only honest place for it to close.
